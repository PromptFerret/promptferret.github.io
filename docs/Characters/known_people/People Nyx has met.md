## People Nyx has Met

> [!summary]- Agrona  
> **Origin & Lineage:**  
> - Hails from **Talos**, a war-ravaged world where conflict is part of daily life.  
> - Member of a **blood-cursed lineage**, a generational affliction likely divine in origin.  
> - Her people, including non-cursed groups like the **Sarkaz**, use **masks** and symbolic items to preserve identity under harsh conditions.  
>
> **Race & Class:**  
> - **Archdruid**, specializing in **Osteomancy** (bone magic) and **Reincarnation**. Her take on death is sacred but not macabre.  
> - Also trains in **monastic traditions**, balancing nature magic with physical and spiritual discipline.  
>
> **Philosophy & Ethics:**  
> - **Consent and morality** are core to her practice: she will only raise someone if the spirit agrees and no innocents are harmed.  
> - Will suggest **science-based resurrection** paths when more fitting, such as the _Morgue_ or _Tempering Starlight_.  
> - **Rejects Ba'Straata**, a questionable scientist possibly interested in changeling DNA.  
> - Displays a profound respect for personal agency, even in magic and death.  
>
> **Religious Affiliation:**  
> - Currently follows **Keranos**, god of storms and inspiration, for pragmatic reasons.  
> - Open and curious toward **Sune's** teachings—especially in love, beauty, and divine pleasure—due to Nyx’s influence.  
>
> **Personality:**  
> - **Stoic**, reserved, and introspective, with a strong sense of emotional control.  
> - Displays dry wit and sarcasm, occasionally teasing those she trusts.  
> - Avoids alcohol (prefers **milkshakes**), valuing control over mind and body.  
> - **Loyal but guarded**—shows affection only after deep trust is earned.  
>
> **Lifestyle & Behavior:**  
> - Avoids rooms at the **Astral Oasis**, instead perching on rooftops, craving solitude.  
> - Her **mask** is both a cultural artifact and a defense mechanism—removing it is an act of intimacy and vulnerability.  
> - Sees the world through a lens of trauma, family duty, and resilience.  
>
> **Relationship with Nyx:**  
> - One of Nyx’s most trusted companions—**accepted their changeling identity** without hesitation.  
> - Shared personal customs and stories, including **mask traditions**, **family trauma**, and her cursed lineage.  
> - Has displayed **deep emotional intimacy**: removed her mask in Nyx’s presence, cuddled, supported, and passionately kissed them.  
> - Gave Nyx a **Mind Flayer’s Skull** for protection; in turn, received a **Tankard of Sobriety** from Nyx.  
> - Jokes about being Nyx’s “future hero,” and calls them “baby” with both humor and sincerity.  
> - Joins Nyx during work, helps them rest, and **protects their emotional and physical well-being**.  
> - Shared a **romantic and physical night** with Nyx on the Crimson Mirage—fully mutual, emotionally and physically intimate.  
> - Supported Nyx’s devotion to **Sune**, offered to help gather followers and assist in building their temple.  
>
> **Notable Events:**  
> - **Helped guide Nyx** after a mission on Talos; after Nyx touched a boss corpse, they gained **crystals in their hand** matching Agrona’s eye color.  
> - **Shared a date** with Nyx that ended in an exhilarating dash across rooftops to the ship where they spent the night together—an act of trust and unity.  
> - Encourages **emotional growth** for herself and others, aiming to overcome her upbringing’s emotional void.

> **Connection to Agrona:**  
> - **Close companion and ally** to Agrona; likely a **comrade-in-arms** or trusted **co-worker**.  
> - Fights **alongside Agrona** in an ongoing war—possibly sharing the same origin world of **Talos**, or a connected front in a broader conflict.  
> - Their bond suggests mutual respect and shared history, forged in hardship or through shared ideology.  
>
> **Relationship with Nyx:**  
> - First heard of Nyx **through Agrona**, indicating that Agrona **spoke positively or significantly** enough about Nyx for Valerie to take interest.  
> - Might be cautious but curious—depending on how much Agrona has shared or the nature of Nyx’s introduction.  
>
> **Role & Personality (Inferred):**  
> - Likely a **warrior or tactician**, given the context of active conflict.  
> - Practical, loyal, and possibly protective of those she trusts (especially Agrona).  
> - Could serve as a **foil to Agrona**—either more openly emotional or more militaristic in contrast to Agrona's philosophical stoicism.  
>
> **Potential Hooks:**  
> - Might act as a gatekeeper or reality check for Nyx if they grow closer to Agrona.  
> - Could become an ally or antagonist depending on Nyx’s actions and values.  
> - Offers an opportunity to explore the broader context of the war Agrona came from.

> [!summary]- Miles  
> **Origin & Backstory:**  
> - Grew up an only child in a happy home with both parents in **Baldur’s Gate**.  
> - His father was a high official whose biggest case involved Miles’s uncle—until his father was killed by his uncle’s boss, in front of Miles.  
> - After this traumatic event, Miles was brought to “the Shores” via a portal from a friend and chose to stay.  
> - An incident during a school field trip to a science lab changed him—now he wields **darkness**, **invisibility**, and **bioelectricity**.  
>
> **Personality:**  
> - **Kind**, gentle, and thoughtful—he consistently affirms Nyx’s happiness and effort.  
> - **Flirtatious**, but clumsy (e.g., “you can eat me any day... that sounded better in my head”).  
> - Respectful and supportive; curious about others and open to sharing his story.  
> - Drawn to meditation and calm, fitting well with the spa setting Nyx curated.  
>
> **Abilities & Traits:**  
> - Can **cast Darkness** and likely has stealth/invisibility powers.  
> - Possibly has Devil’s Sight.  
> - Interested in powers, dimensional spaces, and personal sanctuaries.  
> - Romantic charm tempered by respect for boundaries.  
>
> **Relationship with Nyx:**  
> - Opens with flirting and gentle affection (playing with hair, calling “baby”).  
> - Shares personal trauma to deepen trust.  
> - Admires Nyx’s compassion and consent-driven ethics.  
> - Respects Nyx’s rules and boundaries.  
> - Plans a future **date**, eager to grow closer.
