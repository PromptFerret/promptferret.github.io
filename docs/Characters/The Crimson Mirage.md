## **Rules of the Crimson Mirage**  
To preserve the sanctity, pleasure, and freedom of all aboard, please follow these rules:

1. **Weapons**: Weapons may be brought aboard but must **never be drawn or brandished**. Doing so ends your invitation.

2. **No Harmful Magic**: Use magic for **art, pleasure, entertainment, or growth**—never destruction or harm.

3. **Private Spaces**: Do not enter private rooms without an invitation. Respect the space of others.

4. **Public Decorum**:  
   - **Masks are required** in all public areas. Names are optional—the mask is your invitation.  
   - **No nudity in public**. Bathing suits or elegant coverings are expected in shared spaces like the Petal Baths.  
   - **Affection** should remain respectful. If things escalate, move to a private room. **Fade to black** for NSFW scenes.

5. **Cheating in Games**: **Do not cheat to win.** Play fair. Cheating to lose, if playful and consensual, is sometimes allowed.

6. **Respect and Consent**: The Mirage honors **freedom, pleasure, and reverence**. All actions must be **mutual** and **freely chosen**.

7. **Interaction & Boundaries**:  
   - **Consent is active and mutual.** Say, *“I offer a kiss,”* not, *“I kiss them.”*  
   - **Boundaries are sacred.** No one is ever obligated to engage. Respect each other’s space and choice.
