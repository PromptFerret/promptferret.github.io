
---
This term covers all animal life in the Arcanum Mechanica universe.

Derived from the Latin **_anima_** ("_soul_" or "_life_"), it serves as an inclusive category that unites every animal—reptiles, mammals, birds, amphibians, and other mobile life forms—regardless of complexity or consciousness.

---

### Noemari
>[[Noemari]] comprises those Animari that possess advanced cognitive abilities, self-awareness, and complex communication skills. Rather than being defined by their physical appearance, this group is united by its mental and cultural attributes.
### Naturari
>Naturari encompasses all non-sentient animal life forms within the Animari.
