>**Animari** encompasses all **animal life** within the Arcanum Mechanica universe, ranging from simple, instinct-driven creatures to highly intelligent beasts. This classification includes **reptiles, mammals, birds, amphibians, insects, and all other mobile life forms** that thrive across the world's diverse landscapes. While some Animari may exhibit intelligence or even rudimentary communication, true **sapience**—the ability for abstract thought and self-awareness—is the defining trait of **Sentari**, a separate category reserved for sentient beings.
> 
> _Root:_ Derived from the **Latin** _anima_ ("soul" or "life"), emphasizing the fundamental essence of living, moving creatures.
