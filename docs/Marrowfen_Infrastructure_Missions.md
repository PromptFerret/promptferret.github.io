# 🛠️ Marrowfen Infrastructure Missions

This file contains modular, group-based mechanics for building and gathering efforts in the living world of Marrowfen. These systems are tailored for one-shots (4–5 hours), for 4–6 players in Tier 1 or 2.

---

## 🌲 Clear Trees & Harvest Lumber

**Goal:** Clear a 40 ft. x 40 ft. patch and gather usable wood  
**Labor DC Pool:** 50  
**Phases:** 3 (one per hour/day)

**Valid Checks:**  
- Athletics (STR): Chopping and hauling  
- Nature (INT): Identifying viable trees  
- Survival (WIS): Planning efficient clearing  
- Carpenter’s Tools: Processing wood  
- Spells (e.g. *mold earth*): Automatic 15 once per casting or +5 to another’s check

**Complications (Optional):**  
- Nat 1: Lose progress or minor injury  
- Phase 2: Hidden spider nest, dryad, or cursed stump

---

## 🪨 Quarry & Transport Stone

**Goal:** Gather and ferry foundation stone across the river  
**Labor DC Pool:** 60–75 (based on weather/load)  
**Phases:** 3–4

**On-Site Checks:**  
- Athletics: Mining, carrying  
- Investigation: Finding veins  
- Stonecutter’s Tools  
- Arcana: Magical seams

**Ferry Phase (per trip):**  
- Athletics: Pull the rope  
- Survival: Load balancing  
- Carpenter’s Tools: Patch deck  
- Water Vehicles: Direct ferry  
- Spells: e.g. *floating disk*, *shape water* (auto 10–15 or +bonus)

**Complications (Optional):**  
- Rope frays (on 1)  
- Listing barge (2+ fails)  
- River beast appears (Tier 2)

## ⛏️ Build a Well

**Goal:** Reach groundwater and build a reinforced well  
**Labor DC Pool:** 80  
**Phases:** 4 (scouting, digging, lining, sealing)

**Valid Checks:**  
- Athletics  
- Survival  
- Mason’s Tools  
- Nature  
- Perception  
- Stonecutter’s Tools  
- Spells: *mold earth*, *purify food and drink*, *detect poison* (flat contribution or check bonuses)

**Complications (Optional):**  
- Cave-in risk  
- Poison gas  
- Insects or cursed water  
- Foreshadowing whispers or relics

**Results:**  
- Success (80+): Working well, map update, morale bonus  
- Partial (60–79): Unstable or tainted  
- Failure (<60): Abandoned attempt

## 🌉 Build a Bridge

**Goal:** Construct a simple plank or rope bridge over the river  
**Labor DC Pool:** 75  
**Phases:** 4

**Valid Checks:**  
- Carpenter’s Tools  
- Mason’s Tools  
- Survival  
- Athletics  
- Acrobatics  
- Spells: *levitate*, *floating disk*, etc. for material handling

**Complications (Optional):**  
- Structural failure (failed check by 5+)  
- Materials fall into river  
- Opposing advice among builders

**Results:**  
- Success: Permanent structure on map  
- Partial: Passable but weak  
- Fail: Delays, redo required
